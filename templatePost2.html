Noah Salasnek

Gepost op 26 mei 2016

Noah Salasnek needs no introduction. Noah’s fluid style helped define snowboarding as we know it today. Noah was a true visionary, his snowboarding inspired many. Noah is now faced with the biggest challenge of his life. We’d like to assist him in overcoming this obstacle. SIMS presents The Salasnek Reissue Package. Via lottery and randomly selected, 12 lucky people will be chosen to purchase 1 of the 12 packages which include:

-One signed, made in the USA, individually numbered reissue of Noah’s 1993/94 1510 (The Nub) SIMS Pro Model snowboard.

-One signed, made in the USA, individually numbered, reissue of Noah’s 1993/94 1550 SIMS Pro Model snowboard.

-A new Reissue Package print signed by Derek Johnson, former SIMS Art Director and creator of Noah’s iconic hobo/skate base graphic.

-A Certificate of Authenticity signed by Noah, Mike McEntire, Dave Hatchett, Mike Hatchett, Chris Roach, Monty Roach and Aaron Vincent, founders of the Noah Salasnek Fund.

That’s two signed reissue Noah Salasnek Pro Model snowboards, a signed print, and a signed Certificate of Authenticity. The Salasnek Reissue Package will cost $1800.00 plus shipping. 100% of the net proceeds will go directly to The Noah Salasnek Fund.

If you’re interested in entering the lottery, please send a Facebook message to SIMS stating that you’d like to enter the Salasnek Reissue Package lottery along with your full name, shipping address, email address and phone number. The lottery is open now and will close on June 10th. Winners will be chosen at random and notified of selection. Once you have been notified full payment will be collected. Payments will be collected via credit card. Packages will ship in September.

Help support Noah and let’s show him the love and thanks he deserves. Here’s to you Noah, we know you can stomp this one!
